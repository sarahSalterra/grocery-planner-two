# Grocery Planner Two

A comprehensive household management app that guides you through meal planning, grocery shopping, cooking, and restocking household goods — all in one place.

## Overview

Grocery Planner Two is designed to simplify the full cycle of home food and supply management. From deciding what to cook this week, to building a smart shopping list, to walking you through recipes step by step, to knowing when it's time to restock — this app keeps your household running smoothly.

## Core Features (Planned)

- **Meal Planning** — Plan meals by day or week, with suggestions based on what you already have
- **Grocery Shopping** — Auto-generate shopping lists from your meal plan, organized by store section
- **Cooking Guidance** — Step-by-step recipe instructions with timers and serving adjustments
- **Household Restocking** — Track pantry and household supplies, get alerts when items run low
